Rules

  1. Everyone is at the same location with a different job...
  2. Except for the spy.
  3. The players win when someone calls a vote, it unanimously succeeds, and they've discovered the spy.
  4. The spy wins at any time when they declare they are the spy and guess the correct location.
  5. The spy ALSO wins if time runs out.
  6. Each turn of the game, you may ask any player one question with one answer of any kind. They answer in whatever way they wish and then ask the next question.
  7. You may not ask a question of the person who just asked you a question.

Example Round - Players Alice, Bob, Sue

Alice asks bob "What kind of clothes did you wear to this event?"

Bob thinks the question is a bit generic, so he responds with little information. "My usual work clothes. Sue, are you here as a guest or a patron?"

Sue says "Guest."

Alice and Bob call a vote naming Sue the spy, and they win. Because they were on a nuclear submarine.
